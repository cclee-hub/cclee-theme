Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from our buyers worldwide.

Are your tips compatible with JBC / Hakko / Weller stations?

Yes. Our tips are manufactured to match the original specifications. We produce compatible tips for JBC C210/C245/C470 series, Hakko 900M/FM-20/T12 series, Weller LT/XT series, and many more.

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

For stock items, there is no minimum order. For custom OEM orders, MOQ starts at 500 pieces depending on the product type. Contact us for specific requirements.

Do you offer OEM/ODM services?

Yes, OEM and ODM are our core business. We can print your logo on products and packaging, design custom tip shapes, and develop entire product lines under your brand.

What is the lead time for orders?

Stock items ship within 3-5 business days. Custom OEM orders typically take 15-25 days depending on quantity and specifications. Rush orders available upon request.

Can I get a sample before placing a bulk order?

Absolutely. We encourage samples so you can verify quality first. Sample orders are shipped within 2 days via DHL/FedEx. Sample cost is refundable against your first bulk order.

Need Custom Soldering Tools?

Send us your specs, samples, or current brand model — our engineering team will respond within 24 hours.
